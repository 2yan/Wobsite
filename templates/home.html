Who are we?

Based in the United States, we have a commitment to preserving the financial freedom of American people and businesses. We do a few different things but the main things we do are: Direct business receivable purchases and we also specialize in selecting packages that are tailored to realistically fit the needs of borrowers.

The direct micro-financing

This product tends to take a little more time for approval but we can fund people that no one else would be willing to take the time to look at and provide amazing rates while doing that too. We do this in a few ways:

  • We negotiate with brokers to keep the rates low

    With most other finance providers your broker will take a 10% cut of the total financed amount. We, if the client is applying for our direct micro financing, do not allow the broker to charge such high fees to the client.

  • We don't charge any surprise closing fees.

    Everything is clearly laid out, no surprises.

  • We take the time to analyze each business thoroughly

    Even if you just need $2,000 or even $500 dollars. We take the time to listen and understand your business. We understand that business are made of people and can have different special circumstances. There is not such thing as a restricted industry for us, we will take the time to listen to why your business is going to succeed in its goals, we learn everything we can about you. We don't just make you fit into a simple model that rejects you just because you're from one industry or have some unrelated factor.

  • Our qualification criteria is transparent and simple

    All we need to do is establish if you are making money consistently OR have a sure fire payment coming in and that's it. Even if your revenue isn't consistent, if your revenue is seasonal and we can see evidence of that, we will likely be happy to fund you.
