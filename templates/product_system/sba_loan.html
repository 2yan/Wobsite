Step 3: Obtain the financial documents and records.

Gather the following documents to the best of your ability:

  • Business tax returns for the past 3 years*
  • Personal tax returns for the past 2 years
  • Business bank statements for the past 6 months
  • Current balance sheet and profit & loss statement
  • Shareholders and guarantors form
  • A list of all outstanding debts

* - You don't need 3 years of tax returns if you havve only been in business for a year.
